Stay among the vineyards, not in the neighborhoods

Welcome to Summers Hill Wine Country Retreat

Family retreat and wine tasting getaway sitting atop Summers Hill just west of Carlton. To the east guests look down into forested hills and two fabulous vineyards with Mt Hood in the distance. To the south guests gaze down over grassy farmlands and pastures to "High Heaven Peak" towards McMinnville. To the west a beautiful view of the Trask mountains with the coast range peeking over the top. Sleeps twelve (six couples) comfortably. One of the highlights of this home is the 2nd story wrap-around balcony and expansive deck including hot tub, gas fire pit and outdoor couches. Having a small to medium size gathering? The Bob and Myrna Summers courtyard, covered gazebo with fire pit and cabins are an added bonus.

COVID-19: We are committed to Airbnb’s 5-step enhanced cleaning process—a set of standards developed in partnership with experts, for the times of COVID-19 and beyond.

Overview

In addition to a well-appointed interior, the outdoor living space (including a fire pit) is picturesque and inviting. The hot tub on the deck of the second floor finishes the long, long list of reasons this property is ideal for quiet retreats and small family get-togethers. Peaceful, cozy lounging awaits!

From late nights in the hot tub staring at the stars, to watching deer in the early mornings with a cup of coffee. We are your home away from home.

Whether it is a small get together of friends, or a large family reunion, we have you covered. Enjoy the Bob and Myrna Summers courtyard for all gathering sizes.

With two cabins on the property, we can sleep up to 12 people. Perfect for holiday get aways, and summer get togethers.

About

A three generation family home founded by local Carlton icons; Bob and Myrna Summers have been beloved by the Carlton community since 1969. Wanting to keep this beloved and peaceful property in the family, two of their sons and daughters in law have preserved this beautiful retreat for future generations. Come feel the love!
